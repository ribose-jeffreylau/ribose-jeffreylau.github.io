# About

This registry is designated by ISO/TC 46/WG 3 as the registry for the
[ISO 24229 standard](https://www.iso.org/standard/78143.html).


## Responsibility

The registry is managed by the ISO 24229 Advisory Group (AG) of ISO/TC 46/WG 3.
The convenor of the AG is Mr. Peeter Päll of Estonia.

The online version of this registry is jointly managed by the AG and
[CalConnect](https://www.calconnect.org)/[Ribose](https://open.ribose.com),
the Registration Authority of this registry.
